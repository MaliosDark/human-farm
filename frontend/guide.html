🌌 HUMAN FARM: Game Guide 🌌

Welcome to the wild, dystopian world of HUMAN FARM! Ready to manage, mutate, and exploit your way to galactic domination? Buckle up, because this pixelated strategy game is gonna blow your mind! 🚀

🧠 What's HUMAN FARM?

You're the boss of a creepy bio-engineering facility, creating genetically modified humans to mine Essence, a super-valuable resource. Build your base, upgrade your humans, raid other players, and wrestle with some *seriously* tough moral choices. It’s strategy, it’s dark, it’s pixelated, and it’s FUN!

🎮 How to Play

Jump into the game right in your browser—no downloads needed! Here’s the lowdown:

  • Create Humans: Spawn new humans with unique traits. Each one’s got their own vibe—some are loyal, some are troublemakers!
  • Assign Jobs: Put your humans to work on your 10x10 isometric grid. Send them to Farms (to mine Essence), Labs (to get mutated), or Guard Posts (to fend off raiders).
  • Extract Essence: Collect that sweet, sweet Essence to upgrade your humans or unlock cool tech like auto-collectors or stealth raids.
  • Raid or Defend: Scout other players’ bases on the global map. Steal their Essence or kidnap their humans—but watch out, they can hit you back!
  • Mind the Morality: Overwork your humans, and they’ll rebel. Treat them nice, and they might stick around. Every choice matters!

Pro tip: Keep an eye on your humans’ Fatigue and Morality. Push them too hard, and they might die, revolt, or just run away. 😱

🧬 Humans: Your Creepy Workforce

Each human is unique, with stats like:

  • Essence/hr: How fast they mine.
  • Mutation: Special powers (or curses).
  • Morality: High = loyal, Low = trouble.
  • Fatigue: Too high, and they’re toast.

Upgrade them in the Lab, but don’t push too far—low morality humans might start a Rebellion Event and wreck your base!

🧱 Build Your Empire

Your 10x10 grid is where the magic happens. Build stuff like:

  • Farms: Where Essence gets made.
  • Labs: For cloning and upgrading humans.
  • Guard Posts: To stop pesky raiders.
  • Storage Units: Hold extra Essence.

Everything’s rendered in glorious isometric pixel art, with animations for working humans, glowing Essence, and epic raids!

⚔️ Raid Like a Boss

Feeling sneaky? Use the minimap to scout other players’ bases. If their defenses are weak, send in your raiders to steal Essence or capture humans. But beef up your own Guard Posts, or you’ll be the one crying!

Raids are all about strategy—pick your targets wisely and time your attacks for maximum chaos. 😈

🧪 Morality: The Heart of the Game

Every choice you make affects your humans’ Morality. Overwork them? -15 points. Clone them without consent? -25. Give them a break? +10. Free a human? +30!

If morality drops too low, you’re in for a Rebellion Event. Humans might attack your buildings, stop working, or take over part of your grid. Handle it by negotiating, reprogramming, or... well, you know. 🔫

Complete daily quests, climb the leaderboards, and unlock cool cosmetics or rare humans. Share epic raid replays or show off your unique humans on social media. The game’s dark, but the fun is real!
